Hello there! I'm Gozde, a Toronto-based multidisciplinary UX/UI designer with a background in interior design.


I graduated from Hacettepe University, Faculty of Fine Arts, Department of Interior Architecture and Environmental Design in 2015. For more than 6 years, I have worked as an interior designer in many national and international large-scale projects. Working with different disciplines, I gained the ability to approach human-centered design from different angles, and I decided to adapt this skill from interior architecture to digital product design. For this, I completed the CareerFoundry UI Design Program in 2023. I developed myself in the fields of user experience and user interface design in order to produce solution-oriented projects with high visual impact in this field.


Combining all of my multidisciplinary knowledge and experience so that the end-to-end user experience is seamless, intuitive and visually satisfying is the key to my holistic design approach.
