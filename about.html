Hello there! I'm Gozde, a Toronto-based multidisciplinary UX/UI designer with a background in interior design.


I graduated from Hacettepe University, Faculty of Fine Arts, Department of Interior Architecture and Environmental Design in 2015. For more than 6 years, I have worked as an interior designer in many national and international large-scale projects in collaboration with other disciplines. Working with different disciplines, I gained the ability to approach human-centered design from different angles, and I decided to adapt this skill from interior architecture to digital product design. For this, I completed the CareerFoundry UI Design Program in 2023. I developed myself in the fields of user experience and user interface design in order to produce solution-oriented projects with high visual impact in this field.


As a UX/UI designer who comes from an interior design background, my creativity is sparked by the dynamic energy of the city, the new place exploration, and the cultural richness found in bookstore visits. Through strategic problem-solving and effective communication, I craft designs that not only tell compelling narratives but also provide users with immersive experiences. I integrate my multidisciplinary knowledge to ensure a seamless, intuitive, and visually satisfying end-to-end user experience, making holistic design solutions the cornerstone of my approach.
